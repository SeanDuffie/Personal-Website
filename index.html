About Me

Back to Top

My name is Sean Duffie, I'm majoring in Electronic Systems at Texas A&M University (Class of 2022), with a Computer Science minor. I'm a life long resident of Cypress, Texas. I earned the rank of Eagle Scout through a decade-long regimen that required a substantial amount of effort and commitment. My favorite memories from Scouting were the campouts, which is an activity that I still enjoy today. I competed in cross country (track) and on my community’s swim team while attending junior high and high school. I still enjoy long-distance running and swimming, although I no longer pursue either activity competitively. I enjoy reading, playing games, and spending time outside in my free time. Much of my teen-years involved interacting with computers, but I didn't take my first computer science course until my senior year in high school. That class was initially difficult for me, but it wasn't long before I realized that I had found my passion. My first exposure to programming was learning Java, but I’ve since added Python, C++, HTML, and others to my knowledge-base.
