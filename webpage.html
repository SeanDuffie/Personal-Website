About Me

My name is Sean Duffie, I'm currently an Electronic Systems student at Texas A&M University - Class of 2022 - with a Computer Science Minor. I was born and raised in Cypress, Texas. In high school I ran cross country and participated in swim team, and although I don't compete anymore, I still enjoy to set aside time to go for runs and stay in shape. One achievement I'm proud of is attaining the rank of Eagle Scout. This took lots of time and commitment, but some of my favorite memories were from when we went camping. I find the outdoors to be very relaxing, and in my free time I like to read, play games, and spend time outside. I had a lot of experience with computers growing up, but I didn't actually take my first computer science class until my senior year. At first this class posed a great challenge, but it wasn't long before I knew that I had found my passion. My first programming language to learn was Java, but since then I've added Python, C++, HTML, and others to my toolbelt.
